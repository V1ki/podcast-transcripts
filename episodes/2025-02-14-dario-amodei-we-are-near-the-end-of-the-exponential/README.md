              - ## Summary 摘要
             
              - Dario Amodei thinks we are just a few years away from "a country of geniuses in a data center". In this episode, they discuss what to make of the scaling hypothesis in the current RL regime, how AI will diffuse throughout the economy, whether Anthropic is underinvesting in compute given their timelines, how frontier labs will ever make money, whether regulation will destroy the boons of this technology, US-China competition, and much more.
             
              - Dario Amodei 认为我们距离"数据中心中的天才国度"只有几年时间。在本期节目中，他们讨论了当前 RL 体制下的缩放假说、AI 将如何扩散到整个经济体系、考虑到时间线 Anthropic 是否在算力投入上不足、前沿实验室如何盈利、监管是否会摧毁这项技术带来的福祉、中美竞争等话题。
